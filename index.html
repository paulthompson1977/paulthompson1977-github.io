Gas Safety Certificate

Our gas safe engineers are all on the Gas Safe Register. We do annual gas safety checks for kitchens in schools, restaurants, pubs, hotels and any type of catering establishments or commercial kitchens. We are able to issue certificates so that your business is safe and legal.

Service Contracts

We can provide a variety of service contracts based on your needs. A regular payment means you can pay on a monthly basis and potentially reduce your tax bill. This is much better than having your equipment break down, leaving you with a big bill to pay. Our contracts include - Inspection & service contracts, Parts only contracts, and Fully Comprehensive contracts for all types of commercial kitchens.

CRB approved to work in Schools

All of our engineers are approved by the police to work around children in education or healthcare. They have received CRB (Criminal Records Bureau) checks, which are now also known as DBS checks.

Team Experience

Our team is experienced in servicing and repairing ovens, combi-ovens, fryers, grills, dishwashers and every other kind of kitchen appliance. We have worked as catering engineers for major manufacturers, national chains of food suppliers, and many smaller local businesses.
